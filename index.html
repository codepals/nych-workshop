Who We Are

We are girls in the Lotherton and Lawrence Heights area creating our own community through media.

Lotherton Village Girls Group is a girls only group that works towards building and maintaining our confidence and self-esteem. It is a place that allows us to talk about issues without worrying about the constant judgement of the society around us. Most of all, it is a place where us girls have fun and feel free, open, and unafraid to be ourselves. Lotherton Village Girls Group is a safe zone for females of all ages.
